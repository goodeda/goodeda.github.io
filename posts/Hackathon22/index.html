DHH22 Hackathon Project

By zhixu
Posted 2022-05-30 Updated 2022-05-31 1 min read

Project Background

The project is a topic of Helsinki Digital Humanities Hackathon 2022.The theme is Network of Parlamint. The data include national parliament speeches of 17 european countries. We eventually selected and focus on the Spanish term 12, UK term 57, Slovenian term 7 datasets.

Research question

  1. How is the speeches of MPs and mentions of them are related to the power?
  2. Is there power distribution between male and female speeches, regarding different topics.

My work

My work is mainly about the summary statistics preparations, speech flows of MPs and other visual aids.
